Indian Appetizer Foods


Samosa

Description: Triangular pastry filled with a mixture of spiced potatoes, peas, and sometimes minced meat. It is deep-fried until golden brown and served with mint chutney or tamarind sauce.


Chicken Tikka

Description: Succulent pieces of chicken marinated in a blend of yogurt and aromatic spices, skewered, and cooked in a tandoor (clay oven) or oven. Served with mint chutney.


Papdi Chaat

Description: Crispy fried dough wafers (papdi) topped with boiled potatoes, chickpeas, yogurt, and various chutneys. It's a delightful and tangy street food.

Indian Main Foods


Biryani

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with basmati rice, meat (chicken, mutton, or beef), or vegetables. The rice is typically cooked with a blend of spices, including saffron, and is layered with marinated meat or vegetables before being cooked together.


Chole(Chickpea Curry)

Chole is a North Indian dish made with chickpeas cooked in a spiced tomato-based gravy. It is often paired with bhature (deep-fried bread) or rice.


Dosa

Originating from South India, dosa is a thin, crispy pancake made from fermented rice and urad dal (black gram) batter. It is often served with coconut chutney and sambar (a lentil-based vegetable stew).


Dal Makhani

Dal Makhani is a rich and creamy lentil dish made with black lentils (urad dal) and kidney beans (rajma). It is cooked slowly with butter, cream, and a blend of aromatic spices.


Desserts


Gulab Jamun

Deep-fried milk dumplings soaked in a sugar syrup, flavored with cardamom and rose water. They are often served warm and are incredibly soft and sweet.


Jalebi

Spiral-shaped deep-fried batter soaked in saffron-infused sugar syrup. Jalebi has a crispy texture on the outside and is juicy and sweet on the inside.


Halwa

A sweet dish made from various ingredients like semolina (suji), carrots, or lentils, cooked with ghee, sugar, and flavored with cardamom. It has a dense, pudding-like consistency.


Malai Kulfi

Indian ice cream made with thickened milk, sugar, and various flavorings like cardamom, saffron, or pistachios. It is frozen in molds and has a rich, creamy texture.
