Halwa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup almonds, soaked and peeled
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 cup milk
  • A pinch of saffron strands (soaked in warm milk)
  • A handful of chopped nuts (almonds and cashews)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder

Cooking Instructions

  1. Grind soaked and peeled almonds into a smooth paste.
  2. In a pan, heat ghee and add the almond paste. Cook on low heat until it thickens.
  3. Add sugar, saffron-soaked milk, and continue cooking until the halwa reaches a desired consistency.
  4. Add cardamom powder and chopped nuts. Mix well and serve warm.
